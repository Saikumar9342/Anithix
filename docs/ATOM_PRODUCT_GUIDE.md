# Atom — Product Guide
### How the App Works

> Atom lets you build, manage, and publish your own professional portfolio website — entirely from your phone. No coding, no computer, no design skills needed. You make changes in the app, and your live website updates instantly.

---

## What Is Atom?

Atom is made up of two connected parts that work together as one:

- **The Atom App** — installed on your phone. This is where you do everything: set up your profile, add your work, write your story, and control how your site looks.
- **Your Atom Website** — your public portfolio that anyone can visit online. It's the page you share with recruiters, clients, and your network.

The magic is the connection between them: **anything you change in the app appears on your live website within seconds.** Edit your bio on the bus, and it's updated for the world before you reach your stop.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Download and open the app.** You're greeted by a short animated welcome screen.
2. **Sign in with Google.** One tap — no passwords to remember.
3. **First‑time setup.** New users are walked through a friendly onboarding flow. If you've used Atom before (even on a new phone), it recognizes you and takes you straight to your dashboard.
4. **Guided tour.** A gentle, step‑by‑step tutorial points out the main areas so you know exactly where to start. It quietly disappears once you've completed your first few tasks.

---

## Your Dashboard — The Home Base

When you open the app, your dashboard greets you with a live snapshot of how your portfolio is doing:

- **Visits** — how many people have viewed your site.
- **Projects** — how many pieces of work you've published.
- **Messages** — how many people have reached out to you, with a badge showing unread ones.

From here you can jump into any area of your portfolio with a tap.

---

## Building Your Portfolio

Everything on your website is editable from the app. Each part of your site is its own simple editor:

### Your Introduction (Hero)
The first thing visitors see — your headline, a short tagline, a welcome badge, and buttons that guide people to contact you or view your work.

### Your Story (About)
- A biography in your own words.
- Your location.
- Your education history.
- Your work experience.
- Your interests and links to your social profiles.

### Your Skills
Organize what you're good at into clear categories so visitors instantly understand your strengths.

### Your Work (Projects)
Showcase what you've built. For each project you can add:
- A title and description.
- Your role and the category of work.
- The technologies or tools involved.
- A cover image.
- Links to the live project and its source.

You can add, edit, reorder, and remove projects at any time.

### Your Contact Section
Set how people can reach you, including the message form they'll use to get in touch.

## The Magic Touches

### Build Your Whole Portfolio from a Resume
This is one of Atom's most loved features. **Upload your resume (a PDF), and Atom reads it and fills in your entire portfolio for you** — your bio, education, experience, skills, and even projects. What would take an hour of typing happens in moments. You can then review and polish anything you like.

### One‑Tap Writing Help
Not sure how to phrase something? Tap the enhance button on any text field and Atom rewrites it into polished, professional language for you. Great for turning rough notes into something that reads beautifully.

### A Site That Designs Itself
You don't need to pick colors or fonts. Atom can automatically style your entire website around your photos — pulling colors from your profile picture or project images so everything looks coordinated and intentional. Prefer to decide for yourself? You can choose a light or dark look instead, and reorder the sections of your site however you want.

## Making It Yours — Your Web Address

Your portfolio needs an address people can visit. Atom gives you flexible options:

- **A clean personal link** — claim a unique username so your site lives at a tidy, memorable address.
- **Your own domain** — already own something like `yourname.com`? Connect it, and Atom makes your portfolio appear there automatically, securely, with no extra setup.

You can change or reset your address whenever you want, and share your live link with a single tap.

---

## Staying Connected — Messages & Alerts

When someone visits your website and fills out your contact form, here's what happens:

1. Their message arrives in your **Messages inbox** inside the app.
2. **Your phone instantly alerts you** — with a notification, a sound, and a badge on the app icon — so you never miss an opportunity.
3. You can read each message, mark it as read, and remove ones you've handled.

It's like having a personal assistant that taps you on the shoulder the moment someone wants to work with you.

---

## Speaking Every Language

Atom lets you offer your portfolio in **multiple languages**. You can add languages and provide translated versions of your content, so visitors around the world can read your story in the language they're most comfortable with. Your images and visuals stay consistent across every language — only the words change.

## Works Anywhere, Anytime

- **Use it offline.** You can keep editing even without a connection — your changes sync up the moment you're back online.
- **Reliable and private.** Only you can edit your portfolio. Your account, your messages, and your private details stay yours.
